Privacy Policy – Learning Music Notes

Last updated: April 2026

This app does not actively process any personal data by the developer. The app is designed solely for learning music notes and can be used completely offline.

Local Storage

The app uses a local database (Hive) to store settings (e.g. app options) directly on your device. This data does not leave your device and is not accessible to the developer.

No Active Data Sharing

This app does not transmit any data to own servers or actively to third parties.

Note on System-Related Data Transfers

When using the app, data may be transmitted automatically by the app store (e.g. Google Play or Apple App Store) or the operating system (e.g. during installation, updates, or in-app purchases). The developer has no control over these processes.
